Storyboard

I designed my product to be educational use, so the main users will focus on young children and students. Young people can be educated by my products through appealing interactions and short descriptions. I hope they are willing to use my website by themselves instead of forced by someone, so the overall style will be attractive. Moreover, some college students who work on similar projects can be inspired by my ideas and also give me some feedback about how to improve the interactions in the future. Except for basic educational use, I hope people from all ages will have interests to explore my project and enjoy the content.
