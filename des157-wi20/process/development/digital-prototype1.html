Nearly each part of my page will have a simple interaction to play with. Users can click on the items or use keyboard to control the movement, and I also will include the drag and drop feature. There will be some animation accomplished by sprite sheet or gif. For the main interaction canvas, I want to educate my audiences about the cardboard production process and recycling process through simple interactive game. Basically, the manufacturing process will go through from collect materials, transportation, cutting and finally how they go into people's daily lives. But I am still working on the art of this part so it is hard to show through this prototype.
