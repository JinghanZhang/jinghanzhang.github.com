My idea comes from some side scrolling video games. Many interactions of this kind of video games can be applied to the development of interactive webpage. For my final project, the webpage might be able to scroll down or scroll to right to show the full content and interactions. The left half of the page will be use to display some text, like short stories or descriptions, and the other side will be simple interaction for users to play with the page. Since my project is focus on environmental sustainablility, I can design a garbage sorting interaction, or choose sustainable products to help users better understand the core of environmental sustainablility. All the interactions and descriptions will be connected by the long scrolling background, and users will have a small character to play with the interactions.
